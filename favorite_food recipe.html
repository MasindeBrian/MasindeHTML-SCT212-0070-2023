A RECIPE FOR COOKING PILAU

Pilau Masala is a favorite Kenyan meal and a common delicacy whose roots are
deeply embedded in the culture of the Swahilicommunities
Pilau likely originated in Ancient Persia but Kenyans,and residents of many other
lands â€“ India,Pakistan and most Middle East countries,claim that Pilau originated with them.

INGREDIENTS

  1. 250g Beef
  2. Basmati rice
  3. Vegetable stock
  4. Fresh coriander
  5. Salt and pepper
  6. Oil
  7. Rice Turmeric
  8. Cumin
  9. Cinnamon Sticks
  10. Cardamon pods
  11. Curry leaves

COOKING INSTRUCTIONS

  1. STEP 1:Cut the meat and wash,boil,1 teaspoon of ginger,garlic paste and black pepper,1 grated
    tomato and grated onions
  2. STEP 2:When the meat is tender,drain it and set
    aside.Keep the soup for cooking rice.
  3. STEP 3:Heat the sufuria on a meduim heat and add the oil.then add the whole spices,that is cumin
    pepper,cinnamon and cloves.Once they splutter in the Oil,add chopped onion
  4. STEP 4:Fry them until translucent while stirring them with a spoon.If you want darker pilau,fry the onions
    untilthey are dark and caramelised.Then add turmeric and coriander powder.Let the soup simmer and tase for
    the salt.Make sure you put enough salt for the rice.
  5. STEP 5:Now you cand add boiled meat and stir together for 2 minutes then add some salt and soup.let the
    soup simmer and taste for salt.
  6. STEP 6:when the soup boils , drain the rice and add rice to the soup Sprinkle a bit of coriander on it and stir a
    little.Let it cook on a high heat for 3 minutes then reduce to a medium heat and keep cooking while covered
    until the water finishes.
  7. STEP 7:Make sure you stir every now and then to make sure all the rice is cooked evenly.Then reduce the heat to a very
    low heat and leave it to continue steaming for five minutes. Switch off the heat and serve with Kachumbari
